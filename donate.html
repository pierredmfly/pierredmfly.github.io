Thank You for Using Flight Logger!

Flight Logger is completely FREE with ALL FEATURES unlocked.

No ads. No in-app purchases. No subscriptions. No hidden fees.

If Flight Logger helps you fly safer and log more accurately, please consider supporting development with a PayPal donation:

Why Donate?

Developing and maintaining Flight Logger takes significant time and effort. Your donations help:

  • Keep the app free for all pilots
  • Fund ongoing development and new features
  • Cover Apple Developer Program costs ($99/year)
  • Support testing on multiple Apple Watch models
  • Provide fast bug fixes and updates
  • Respond to user feedback and feature requests

What You Get For Free

  • Real-time G-force monitoring for aerobatics (reference data)
  • Precise flight time tracking with 3 calculation methods
  • Configurable G-force alerts with haptic feedback
  • Complete fuel logging (added and remaining)
  • 7 activity types with customizable trigger rules
  • Detailed flight history (last 10 flights)
  • Workout Session to keep app active during flight
  • Hobbs meter tracking
  • Passenger count recording
  • Complete timestamps for all flight phases
  • No data collection or privacy concerns
  • No ads or tracking

How to Donate via PayPal

  1. Open PayPal or your PayPal app
  2. Select "Send Money"
  3. Enter: DevOps@owwing.fr
  4. Choose your donation amount
  5. Add a note like "Flight Logger Support" (optional)
  6. Send!
